Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ varies by product and factory. We can work with small trial orders (100-500 units) and scale up once you're confident in the product quality.

How do you ensure product quality?

We conduct factory audits before production, in-process inspections during manufacturing, and final quality checks before shipment. You receive photo and video evidence at each stage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We support T/T bank transfer, Western Union, and escrow services for larger orders. We're flexible and work with your preferred payment terms.

How long does sourcing usually take?

Initial quotes are provided within 24 hours. Factory verification and sampling typically take 5-10 business days. Production timelines depend on order complexity but we keep you updated throughout.

Can you help with shipping and customs?

Yes — we coordinate sea freight, air freight, and express shipping. We also prepare all necessary export documentation and can connect you with customs brokers at the destination.

What if I'm not satisfied with the product?

We work with you throughout the sourcing process to ensure specifications are clear before production. If issues arise, we mediate with the factory to find a fair resolution.
